Industrial Elevators

Industrial Elevators, priced at ₹2,70,000, stand out as a reliable solution for heavy-load lifting in factory environments. Manufactured with a commitment to trust, these elevators offer durability and reliability, ensuring they meet the demands of industrial applications.

With a capacity ranging from 3-4 tons, these elevators are designed to handle the challenges of lifting substantial loads in factory settings. Constructed with Mild Steel, they embody strength and robustness, providing a sturdy solution for material handling.

Features:

Equipped with an overloading alarm, these elevators prioritize safety, alerting operators in case of potential overloading. The polished finishing not only adds to the aesthetics but also contributes to the durability of the elevators.
