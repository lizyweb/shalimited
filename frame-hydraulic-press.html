C-Frame Hydraulic Press

The SHAC-Frame Hydraulic Press, priced at ₹3,80,000 per piece, is designed for diverse industrial applications, offering reliability and efficiency. Constructed from durable mild steel, this hydraulic press is characterized by a polished and coated finish, ensuring longevity and resistance against wear and tear.

Featuring fully automatic operation, this hydraulic press is capable of handling a variety of tasks such as bending, pressing, assembling, straightening, punching, riveting, and broaching. Its model name/number, SHA-HRMP-250T, indicates its impressive 250-ton capacity, making it suitable for heavy-duty applications.

Features:

With a control type specified as Non-CNC, this hydraulic press prioritizes simplicity and efficiency in its operation. The hydraulic power source ensures consistent and reliable performance, adding to its overall efficiency.
